Organisation Overview
Labour Movement | Workforce Development & Worker Protection

The National Trades Union Congress (NTUC) is Singapore's pre-eminent labour movement, representing over one million members through 58 affiliated unions, seven affiliated associations, and a portfolio of social enterprises. With approximately 1,200 employees at its central secretariat, NTUC champions workers' rights and interests while delivering essential services through its social enterprise network including FairPrice, NTUC Health, NTUC LearningHub, and NTUC First Campus.
